Bhimashankar Temple

Bhimashankar is an ancient shrine situated in the sahyadri hills of mahrashtra. Bhimashankar temple is a well known Jyotirlinga. Bhimashankar has been declared as a “Wildlife Sanctuary”. A rare animal Malabar Giant Squirrel called as “shekaru” is found in the deep woods. T he Bhimashankar temple is a composite of old and new structures in the nagara style of architecture. It is a modest and graceful temple and it dates back to the 13th century while the sabhamandap was built in the 18th century. The Great Maratha ruler Shivaji is said to have made endowments to this temple to facilitate worship services.
